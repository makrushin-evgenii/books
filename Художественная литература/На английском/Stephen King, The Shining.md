### New words
**hearken**: (verb) to listen
> to **hearken** to the sound

~~**waltzers**: (noun) a form of entertainment in an amusement park which consists of a large machine with a number of parts like cars on it that you sit in and that spin around while moving in a large circle around a central point and going up and down~~
**waltzers**: (???) people who dance waltz?
> and thus the **waltzers** perforce ceased their evolutions

**perforce**: (adverb) because it is necessary
> and thus the waltzers **perforce** ceased their evolutions

**disconcert**: (verb) to make someone feel suddenly uncertain and worried
> and there was a brief **disconcert** of the whole gay company

**chimes**: (noun) a musical instrument consisting of a set of metal tubes that are hit to produce a ringing sound
> while the **chimes** of the clock yet rang

**giddy**: (adjective) feeling silly, happy, and excited and showing this in your behaviour
> ... the **giddiest** grew pale ...

**reverie**: (nount) a state of having pleasant dream-like thoughts
> ... passed their hands over their brows as if in confused **reverie** or meditation
